Though he aspired more than anything to become an Architect, he had to say,"I have to aside with my Engineers, on this one, Arch-Archtect Murd".

Mr. Murd blinked furiously, unsure how to reply to the young man, given the gaggle of Lesser Architects who had arrived to listen. Architect Aspirants did not ever side with anyone, save for the Arch-Architect, whoever that might be on any given day. These days. it was Murd.
